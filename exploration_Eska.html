2015

Festival Pop-up Shop

Concept of a pop-up shop for Eska, a water company,
for a summer festival in Montreal

Summary

Ephemeral structure of 10.75 m long by 6 m high realized as part of a fictional festival on the Place des Festivals in downtown Montreal.

My Role

Researching and understanding the image of the brand, developing the technical plans, designing and rendering the 3D model

Tools

Solidworks, C4D, Photoshop

Details

The shape of the wall is inspired by glaciers (the source of Eskaâ€™s water). The project consisted of understanding the image of the brand and then creating a concept by harmonizing with it. The technical development of the structure has been materialized by producing a series of technical plans, assembly guidelines, in addition of usage scenarios to demonstrate the realism of the project.

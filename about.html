About Simon

Designing New Experiences
in Service, UX & Product Design.

Hi, I'm Simon.

I'm a service and product designer who is passionate about solving problems to create better user experiences, both in person and online.

I am interested in design approaches that involve user participation, and I believe that involving people throughout the design process is beneficial in generating innovative ideas that better meet the needs of the users. I am methodical and meticulous in all of my projects, doing my best to ensure that everyone's voice is heard when it comes to collaborating with others.

In my free time, I enjoy photography, cooking, creating new cocktails, learning about new technologies, being outdoors, traveling and playing with my rescue dog Boba.
